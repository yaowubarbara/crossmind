// PROBLEM

The Blind Spot

Every trading bot optimizes for profit.
Nobody asks when the bot should NOT trade.

Most trading systems fail not because they cannot enter trades,
but because they cannot manage loss.

No audit trail. No refusal logic. No survival constraint.
// SOLUTION

Survival First

  • Survival first, profit second — capital preservation is the prime directive
  • Risk-based refusals are auditable, not silent failures
  • Every decision recorded in a SHA-256 hash-chained Trust Ledger
  • AI-powered adversary stress-tests every signal before execution
"CrossMind does not just execute trades.
It proves why it refused."
